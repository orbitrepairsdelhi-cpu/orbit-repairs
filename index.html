WHAT WE REPAIR

Ball screw services, done right

We work only on ball screws — so every job gets full attention, not a side-task between unrelated machine repairs.

INSP

Inspection & Diagnosis

We measure backlash, preload and wear before quoting — no guesswork, no surprise charges.

CLN

Recirculation Channel Cleaning

Flush and clear the ball return path of debris, dried grease and contamination.

RGR

Regreasing

Full strip-down regreasing to restore smooth, quiet travel and reduce wear.

RPR

Repair & Refurbishment

Rework worn raceways and restore proper screw-to-nut fit and finish.

NUT

Ball Nut Replacement

Swap a damaged ball nut without the cost of replacing the entire screw assembly.

PLD

Preload Adjustment

Restore rigidity and eliminate axial play for accurate, repeatable positioning.
